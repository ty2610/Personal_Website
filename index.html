Web Development Projects

To Do Tracker

A dynamic to do table that allows users to organize their tasks. Custom categories can be created and given their own color. The table can be sorted by clicking the table headers. A visualization in the form of a pie chart is displayed below the table. Progress is saved on a cookie for the next visit.

../Productivity_Web_Site/index.html

Photo Gallery

This is a collection of inspirational pictures, which can be clicked to mimic a lighthouse effect. When the image is enlarged, the selection of pictures are presented, which can be clicked to be enlarged. The play button will start cycle through the pictures every 10 seconds.

Click A Sketch

A basic form of Paint. Hover over the right colors to select a background, and hover over the left colors to change the color of the paint brush. Click the colors to type in a new color. Click the white canvas to begin painting, and click again to stop painting.
